About Us

Who We Are

Good things for people and nature. This thinking has now become a natural part of our lives. There are now many organic and sustainable products and ideas. However, living a lifestyle that incorporates such things may seem a little too strict for us, who struggle to get by in the modern world. We want to provide a lifestyle where we can enjoy incorporating healthy and good things into our lives, as we live in the future with many different faces.

Our Mission

To contribute to the achievement of global goals such as the Paris Agreement and the SDGs through its business, in 2019, Green Goods Emporium formulated its long-term environmental goal of 'Making sustainable lifestyles more accessible'. In 2022, as countries around the world moved to raise their targets, we updated its goal to contribute to the achievement of carbon neutrality by 2050. We will contribute to the realisation of a decarbonised society and a resource-recycling society by promoting the creation of sustainable lifestyles together with consumers, in other words, by providing 'earth-friendly lifestyles' and making lifestyles sustainable without strain.
