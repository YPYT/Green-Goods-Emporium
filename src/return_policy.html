Returns Policy

In accordance with our refund policy, you can receive a refund within 10 days of purchase:

1. If the product is different from the description given beforehand
2. If the quality of the product is extremely poor
3. If the product cannot be delivered due to a fault

If any of the above apply to you, please contact us using the contact form.

In other cases, depending on the circumstances, a refund may be possible. However, please note that we do not offer refunds under the following conditions.

Cases where it is difficult to apply the refund policy:

1. You have lost interest in the product due to a change of heart
2. You no longer need the product after purchasing it
3. You purchased the product by mistake
4. The product is not what you expected

All refunds will be made using the same method of payment as the original purchase. Please note that all refunds will be made in the same way as the original purchase. We do not accept returns for any reason other than the above.

Other than this, we do not accept returns or exchanges for products, except in the case of defective products.

Please make sure that the product you are planning to purchase is the one you want before placing your order.

In the case of defective products, products subject to a manufacturer's recall, or incorrect deliveries, we will replace the product or refund the purchase price (in the case of products that are no longer available, etc., we will not be able to replace the product).

We do not accept returns or refunds for customer convenience after shipment, but if you really want to, the following fees will be charged. In that case, please note that we will not handle returns or refunds if you send the product to our store without contacting us.
