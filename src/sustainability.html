Sustainability

Our Sustainable Activity

Green Goods Emporium is a company that is highly regarded internationally, having been selected for seven years in a row for the Dow Jones Sustainability World Index, a leading global socially responsible investment (SRI) index. We has also been at the cutting edge of reducing environmental impact, such as by launching refillable film containers in the 1990s.
We introduce ideas, activities and products here that we are doing for sustainable living.

We start selling sustainable smart holders!

Smart Holders are revolutionary products that make it unnecessary to refill bottles. We are selling these products in countries where the habit of refilling is not yet established, and are promoting a reduction in environmental impact on a global scale.

Film container recycling

In relation to the recycling of film containers, made from composite materials and difficult to recycle from container to container, we are working to establish a system for collecting them and jointly developing recycling technology. We are promoting initiatives that transcend the boundaries of individual companies, with the aim of accelerating the transition to a resource-recycling society both in Japan and overseas.

News from the shop

In order to conserve our limited resources for the future, we have stopped offering free paper shopping bags at the end of February 2024. We will continue to encourage customers to bring your reusable bags. From March 1st we will offer eco-friendly bags made from recycled cotton for a charge.
