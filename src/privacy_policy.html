Privacy Policy

Last Updated 14 December 2024

We are fully aware that your personal information (name, address, etc., information that can identify an individual) should be handled with care in accordance with the principle of respect for the individual, and we will strive to collect, use and protect it appropriately, as well as to manage it strictly.

1. Acquisition of Personal Information

When we ask for personal information from our customers, we will clearly state the purpose for which it will be used, and will not use it for any other purpose. We will also acquire personal information in an appropriate manner, without deception or other improper means.

2. Use of Personal Information

We will maintain the accuracy and currency of the personal information we have acquired, and will use it within the scope necessary to achieve the specified purpose of use.

3. Safe management of personal information

Our company will take necessary and appropriate measures to prevent the leakage, loss or damage of personal information handled by us, and to ensure the safe management of personal information.

4. Entrusting of personal information

When entrusting the handling of personal information to a third party, we will select a party that meets sufficient standards for the protection of personal information, and will provide necessary and appropriate supervision of the entrusted party to ensure safe management.

5. Providing personal information to third parties

Except in cases where the Personal Information Protection Law or other laws and regulations require it, we will not provide personal information to third parties without first obtaining the consent of the individual concerned.

6. Disclosure, correction, etc. of personal information

If the individual concerned requests the disclosure, correction, or suspension of use of his or her personal information, we will promptly take action after confirming the identity of the individual concerned, except in cases where the law requires otherwise.

7. Disposal of Personal Information

We will dispose of personal information as quickly and safely as possible once the period of time required by our company has passed. If you have any requests or inquiries regarding the handling of personal information by our company, please contact us here.
